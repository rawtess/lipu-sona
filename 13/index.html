~ particles

󱥄 o - (vocative, imperative, or optative)

lesson

~ commands

o is used instead of li when issuing commands, or describing wishes or desires.

jan o pona

~ people should be good

when used with mi or sina, you still have to include o

mi o lape

~ i should sleep

in direct commands to the listener, you can omit sina

o moku pona

~ eat well!

~ addressing people

you can put o after a subject to address them.

soweli Sijala o, sina sona ala sona toki pona?

~ hey tiara, do you know toki pona?

waso o!

~ hey bird!
