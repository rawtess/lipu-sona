lesson

~ interjections!

interjections are sentence fragments which convey a meaning

any word can be used on its own as an interjection indicating the presence of said word

ike a

~ (that's) really bad

jaki!

gross!

any command with o which consists of only a predicate can also be turned into an interjection by removing the o

moku pona

~ eat well!

~ have a nice meal!

kama pona

~ welcome
