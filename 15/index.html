particles

󱤡 la - (between the context phrase and the main sentence)

lesson

~ la

the particle la is used to establish context. whatever comes before la is established as context for whatever comes after. this can be used for a lot of things, and it's something that might take a bit of practice to fully get.

la can be used to indicate time, form an if/then relationship, and more.

~ examples

sina lape la mi kin li lape

~ if you sleep, i'll sleep too

mi la ale li pona

~ everything is fine with me / in my opinion, everything's good

tenpo lon la jan mute li kama

~ right now, lots of people are coming

pilin sina la mi suwi ala suwi?

~ do you think i'm cute? (according to your feelings, am i cute?)

waso anu pipi la, seme li pona mute?

~ are bugs or birds better? (in the context of bugs or birds, which is really good)
